How do you buy as many stocks as you want at half price?

This is a very interesting technique for someone who cannot afford to invest a lot as it allows them to leverage their funds, and trade with much less money but still have the same profit potential as someone with larger account. And the secret for that is "Deep in the money Options". So instead of purchasing a stock, you want to purchase "Deep in the money Call Option". The key is to look at the Delta.

It needs to be as close to 1 as possible. It means that if the share goes up in price by a dollar – the option would go up by almost the same amount. That gives you a chance to have same gains as owning a share but instead you would invest a lot less in the option contracts.

So, if you buy the "Deep in the money Call Options", you would do that instead of buying a stock, and you would still protect your trade with a Put. So, for example, if you would buy "Deep in the money 11 strike Call Options" at 8 $ per contract, you would also buy 1,- 19 strike Put Contract to protect your trade.
